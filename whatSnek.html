What Snek?

Well, snek is an internet meme, usually used when describing cute snake photos. I just use it because I thought it'd be funny.

What is this "Snake" Game?

Snake is a simple video game of which you control a snake like being, navigating through the map to get food, while avoiding the walls and your growing tail. Originally popularized by its appearance in old Nokia mobile phones, now it has become a nostalgic game for a lot of people.
