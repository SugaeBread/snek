Who Snek?

Snek is snek, snek likes snoot boops. Boop the snoot.


Who am I?

I am Kyle Wan, an indie Unity game developer. I love retro games and learning about technology that can aid my game development journey. I make games to pay tribute to those that came before me.


Who did I learn from?

For this project, even though I would give myself most of the credit. I did get inspiration and help from different internet sources.

Code Bullet is the YouTuber that originally gave me the idea of making a snake game. Though he shows some codes of how he makes the entire game in his video, I did not take reference to any of his codes.

W3School tutorials provided me a lot of basic knowledge on producing a HTML game. Components like canvas, player, update and movement are things that I would never figure out on my own.

The folks at the Stack Overflow forum really helps me when I have some specific problem that I can't solve. And it reminds me that the problems I am having now have already been encountered and solved by people before me.
